Two ways to play

Solo Mode

Race the clock and try to clear all cards as fast as you can. Beat your own best time and climb the in-game record list.

Two-Player Mode

Take turns, score points, and see who can keep a streak of correct answers. Perfect for siblings, classmates, or parent-child play.
